Communication Without Exposure

nullcord is a zero-knowledge, encrypted communication system designed for absolute privacy. No logs, no identities, no traceable metadata — just secure, direct communication between your machines.

Core Capabilities

Zero Knowledge

Servers relay encrypted data only. No visibility into users, messages, or relationships.

Multi-Hop Routing

Traffic is routed through layered relays to prevent origin tracing and network correlation.

No Storage

No logs, no disk writes, no persistence. All data exists only in volatile memory.

Ephemeral Identity

Rotating identifiers and key resets eliminate long-term tracking.

Secure Transfer

Encrypted file transfer with integrity validation and zero exposure.

Minimal Core

Lightweight raw socket architecture for maximum performance and control.

Why Choose nullcord

Privacy First by Design

Unlike many communication platforms, nullcord is engineered from the ground up with privacy as a core principle, not an added feature.

No Data Dependency

Many services rely on stored user data to function. nullcord operates without persistent storage, reducing exposure risks entirely.

Minimal Attack Surface

With a lightweight, custom-built protocol and no unnecessary components, nullcord minimizes potential vulnerabilities.

User-Controlled Identity

Transparent Architecture

Designed for Sensitive Use
